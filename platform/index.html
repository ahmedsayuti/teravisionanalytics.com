Satellite & AI Fusion

TerraVision ingests and harmonizes data from every major Earth‑observation constellation. Our proprietary fusion engine combines optical, thermal, and SAR bands into a single analytic surface that reveals crop stress, soil moisture, and pest pressure before they become visible.

  • Sub‑meter resolution for precision agronomy
  • Near‑daily revisit across CONUS
  • Automated atmospheric correction & cloud masking
  • NDVI, EVI, NDWI, and custom indices on demand

At the edge

On‑Device AI Inference

No round‑trip to the cloud. Our quantized deep‑learning models run directly on ruggedized edge nodes in the field — detecting disease, counting plants, and classifying pests with sub‑200 ms latency, even in offline conditions.

  • NVIDIA Jetson Orin & Raspberry Pi CM4
  • Models trained on 4M+ annotated agricultural images
  • Federated learning updates without cloud dependency
  • 98.7% disease‑detection accuracy in field trials

Spatial intelligence

Real‑Time GIS Dashboard

Our web‑GIS interface is built for analysts, agronomists, and policymakers. Vector tile overlays, streaming telemetry, and interactive filtering let you drill from national overview down to individual field boundaries in seconds.

  • MapLibre GL with custom vector tiles
  • PostGIS + TimescaleDB for time‑series geodata
  • Dynamic NDVI / drought / yield heat maps
  • Exportable to GeoJSON, KML, and Shapefile
