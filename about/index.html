What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Democratize geospatial intelligence by delivering real‑time, AI‑powered insights that enable every farmer, agency, and agribusiness to make data‑informed decisions — preserving resources, increasing yields, and securing the global food supply.

Our Vision

A world where satellite imagery, drone telemetry, and edge AI seamlessly connect every acre of farmland to a living digital twin — empowering humanity to anticipate risks, adapt to climate change, and nourish a growing planet.

Our Journey

From Startup to Global Infrastructure

2018

Founded by Gideon Olawale Sodipo & USDA Researchers

TerraVision Analytics was born inside a USDA research lab, combining decades of remote‑sensing expertise with a radical vision to bring edge AI to every field.

2020

First USDA NASS Contract

Deployed our edge‑native platform across 12 million acres of Midwest cropland, delivering weekly crop condition reports at sub‑field resolution.

2022

Series A & Multi‑Agency Adoption

Secured $28M in funding, expanded to 14 federal and state agencies, and processed over 500 million daily AI inference events.

2024

Continental Scale & FedRAMP Authorization

Achieved FedRAMP Moderate compliance, scaled to 2.4 billion acres under surveillance, and released the public REST/GraphQL API.

2026

Building the Future of Food Security

Expanding globally with climate‑adaptation models, carbon sequestration analytics, and next‑generation on‑device AI for smallholder farms.
